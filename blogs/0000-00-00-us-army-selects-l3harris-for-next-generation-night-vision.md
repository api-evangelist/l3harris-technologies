L3Harris Technologies has been selected by the U.S. Army to provide its NOVA™ night-vision goggle system to support the Binocular Night Observation Device (BiNOD) program. The Army awarded a seven-year contract worth up to $465 million to L3Harris, one of several companies chosen to support the program.
