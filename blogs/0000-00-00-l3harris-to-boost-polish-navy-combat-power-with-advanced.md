L3Harris Technologies has been selected to support the Polish Navy’s operational capabilities and maritime defence with an advanced ship management system for the new Miecznik-class frigates. PGZ Stocznia Wojenna, the state-owned shipyard, selected L3Harris’ Integrated Platform Management System (IPMS) to enhance ship performance, reliability and safety.
