The Government of Canada has awarded MAS, an L3Harris Technologies company, two contracts with an initial combined value of approximately $1.1 billion to provide aircraft fleet sustainment and materiel support for this capability, aligned with Canada’s defence priorities, including Arctic sovereignty and allied operations.
